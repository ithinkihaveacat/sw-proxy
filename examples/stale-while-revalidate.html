Stale While Revalidate

This strategy transforms the headers of the following images so that they get a cache-control header of max-age=10, stale-while-revalidate=50.

This has the behavior:

  • image was retrieved less than 10 seconds ago - cached image is returned
  • image was retrieved between 10 and 60 seconds ago - cached image is returned immediately, but service worker retrieves a fresh copy of the image in the background and uses it to refresh the cache
  • image was retrieved more than 60 seconds ago, or not in cache - image is retrieved from network
